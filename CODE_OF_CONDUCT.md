# Code of Conduct

The Health Game community is committed to a respectful, constructive, and
inclusive project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive technical feedback with care and specificity.
- Keep discussions focused on the work and the project goals.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for the impact of your words and
  actions.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about project scope, priorities, and standards.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or attention.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith argument.
- Publishing another person's private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact a project maintainer
privately. If the repository is hosted on GitHub and private reporting is
enabled, use that channel.

Reports should include:

- What happened.
- Where and when it happened.
- Who was involved.
- Any supporting context, links, or screenshots.

Maintainers will review reports promptly and take action they determine is
appropriate, including warnings, temporary restrictions, removal from project
spaces, or escalation to platform administrators.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project repositories, issue trackers, pull
requests, discussions, community spaces, and any public setting where someone is
representing the project.
